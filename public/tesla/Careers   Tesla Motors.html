Careers at Tesla Motors

Tesla Motors designs and manufactures the most advanced electric vehicles and electric powertrains in the world. We do not compromise on innovation, performance, or design. Nor do we compromise our commitment to hiring and developing the best talent from around the world.
We work in small, focused teams that are agile, efficient, and focused on excellence. We are driven by work with global impact and are passionate about building the industry’s best automobiles. The pace is fast, the work is stimulating, structure is limited, and innovation is expected.
 
Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, Tesla has satellite offices in Los Angeles and Amsterdam plus over one hundred retail and service locations around the world.
